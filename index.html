Why this routine?

Hockey + desk work = tight hip flexors and a shortened psoas. This causes knee pain, lower back issues, and reduced stride power. This routine targets the root cause, not just the symptoms.

The psoas muscle

The psoas connects your spine to your legs. When it shortens from sitting or skating in a crouched position, everything else compensates. The daily routine here addresses this directly.

How to use this

Open each exercise, watch the video, use the timer. Tap "Done" when finished. Dynamic exercises before skating or running warm the tissue. Static stretches after lengthen it. Both matter.

Sequence matters

The exercises are ordered intentionally. Dynamic before static. Don't skip ahead. The muscle needs to be warmed before it can be lengthened. Consistency over weeks beats intensity in one session.
